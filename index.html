Adoption Stories

Annie decides to go to a pet shelter to find a puppy as a new member of her family. When she entered the shelter, she saw many cute puppies, but one in particular caught her attention, a yellow and white fluffy puppy. Annie was immediately impressed by the puppy's innocent eyes and cheerful personality, and she decided to adopt him. She named the dog "Kiki" and took him home with her. Kiki quickly adapted to her new family, and she and Annie became best friends. They walk, play, and care for each other. Annie gives Kiki a warm, safe home, and Kiki gives Annie a lot of love and joy.

Annie and Kiki 's Family

When Jack enters the shelter, he is attracted by a puppy with charming eyes and a furry appearance. This puppy is called "QQ" and she looks very lively and friendly. Jack knows that this is the companion he has always dreamed of, so he decides to adopt Qiaoqiao. Qiaoqiao becomes Jack's best friend and they embark on a fantasy adventure together.
